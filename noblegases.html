Helium


Atomic Mass: 4
Atomic Number: 2

Helium is used for filling balloons (blimps) and for pressurizing liquid fuel rockets. Mixtures of helium and oxygen are used as an artificial 'air' for divers and others working under pressure.

Neon


Atomic Mass: 20
Atomic Number: 10

When a few thousand volts are applied to neon, it emits an orange/red light. It is therefore often used in brightly lit advertising signs. Neon is also used in high-voltage warning indicators, in Geiger counters and in television tubes. Liquid neon is used as a cryogenic refrigerant.
